# Paragliding Landing Spots Coordinates

## 1. Monte Baldo - Malcesine, Lake Garda, Italy
*   **Location:** Malcesine Landing Zone (on the shores of Lake Garda, ~2km north of Malcesine)
*   **Coordinates:** 45.782167, 10.817833 (45°46.930'N 10°49.070'E)
*   **Notes:** Managed by Paragliding Club Malcesine. Life jacket mandatory.

## 2. Col Rodella - Campitello di Fassa, Dolomites, Italy
*   **Location:** Ischia Landing (near Col Rodella cable car valley station)
*   **Coordinates:** 46.4766, 11.7435 (Approximate landing zone based on cable car station proximity)
*   **Notes:** Watch out for power lines. Windsock visible.

## 3. Interlaken, Switzerland
*   **Location:** Höhematte Park (Central Interlaken)
*   **Coordinates:** 46.686409, 7.859877
*   **Notes:** Large green space in the center of town. Popular for tandem landings.
